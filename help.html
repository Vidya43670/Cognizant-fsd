Community Portal Help Center

How to Register for an Event

  • Go to the Events section.
  • Enter your Name and Email.
  • Select an Event.
  • Choose a Date.
  • Click the Register button.

How to Submit Feedback

  • Scroll to the Feedback section.
  • Enter your Phone Number.
  • Write your feedback.
  • Click Submit Feedback.

Find Nearby Events

Click the "Find Nearby Events" button and allow location access. The portal will display your current coordinates.
